Philosophy

Every decision
has a reason.

01
Wrist-first
If a phone app does it better, we don't build it. Every feature earns its place on your wrist.
02
Haptic language
All apps share one haptic ecosystem. Signals have internal structure — they feel deliberate, not like notifications.
03
No subscription
One-time purchase. No recurring fees, no pay-to-win, no deception. You buy it, you own it.
04
Privacy by design
No cloud dependency for core features. Behavioral data never leaves your device. Presence is consent-based.
05
Battery honest
No unjustifiable always-on drain. Every background process has a measurable benefit that justifies its cost.
06
Solo built
One developer. Every feature is scoped to what can be built and maintained with full attention to quality.
