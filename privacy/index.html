Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

JellyToot ("we", "our", "us") builds Wear OS apps. This policy explains how our apps handle your data.

The Short Version

Our apps store all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data to any third party. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no cloud backends in our core apps.

Data We Store Locally

  • Sixth: Orbit member names and connection identifiers, saved place fingerprints (WiFi and BLE signal data), Focus and Mute preferences. All stored on-device only.
  • WatchKeys: Your keyboard layout preferences. Stored on-device only.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect usage analytics
  • We do not collect crash reports automatically
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not sell any data to any party

Bluetooth and Location Permissions

Our apps request Bluetooth and Location permissions required by Android for BLE scanning and advertising. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data Sync Between Watch and Phone

Sixth syncs orbit member data between your paired watch and phone using Android's Wearable Data Layer — a direct device-to-device connection that does not route through our servers.
